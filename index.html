AMAR E SOFRER

four songs that never miss — four shades of love

Four songs, each revealing a different shade of love — its tenderness, its ache, its intensity, its quiet acceptance.

Together, they form a cycle that's easy to return to, no matter the day or mood.

Track 01
The Spark

Amor de Novo

NAPA & Jovem Dionisio

That First Jolt

That feeling when you meet someone and everything just click. This is the track for the beginning — when you're giddy, hopeful, and slightly terrified of how much you already like them. It's pure, uncomplicated, new love energy. You just can't stop smiling.

Track 02
The Obsession

Shinunoga E-Wa

Fujii Kaze

All-In Love

This is when it stops being a crush and becomes a forever promise. It's intense, dramatic, and a little crazy, but you mean every word. Seriously, the song says it all: you'd literally rather die than not have them. It's your ride-or-die anthem.

Track 03
The Break

Nerves

DPR IAN

When It Falls Apart

The brutal aftermath. This song is the sound of your heart breaking, capturing that deep, sickening feeling when you're left with nothing but shaking nerves and the raw, piercing ache of the loss. It's okay to cry to this one, especially when the moodswings hit hardest.

Track 04
The Acceptance

Amar pelos Dois

Salvador Sobral

Enduring Sacrifice

This is the final stage — when you learn that true love is often about giving more than you get. It's a tender, hopeful track about loving them so much that you're ready to "love for two". It's the mature, selfless affection that sticks around long after the drama fades.
